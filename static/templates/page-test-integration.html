Academics at Widener

When you enroll in an undergraduate or graduate academic program at Widener University, you’re taking the first step toward achieving your career and life goals.

College students of all ages love Widener for its academic quality, access to professors, and focus on experiential learning, leadership development, career preparation, and community service.

 

Degree Programs at Widener

No matter where you are in your studies, Widener has a program for you. Check out our academic programs to see which one fits you best.

Not Sure Yet What You Want To Study?

Widener academics offerings are distributed across several individual schools and centers of learning. Our academic programs include over 60 undergraduate majors and minors and numerous graduate and professional degrees.

  • School of Business Administration
  • College of Arts & Sciences
  • Center for Continuing Studies
  • School of Engineering
  • Center for Education
  • Center for Hospitality Management
  • Center for Human Sexuality Studies

  • Institute for Graduate Clinical Psychology
  • Institute of Physical Therapy Education
  • Delaware Law School
  • Commonwealth Law School
  • School of Nursing
  • Center for Social Work Education

 

Come Visit Us!

To learn more about Widener's undergraduate programs, schedule a visit or speak with an admissions counselor.

For Widener's graduate programs and certificates, drop in for one of our open houses or contact us in the Graduate Admissions Office at gradprograms@widener.edu.
